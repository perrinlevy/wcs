Have a question?
We're here to answer

How best to work through the program's sections?

The sections must be completed sequentially. As such, we recommend setting aside a 2-3 hour time block every weekend so you can consistently work through the material. At that pace, it would take approximately 16 weeks to fully complete the program.

How do I know if I'm a good fit for the program?

Many of our clients are either recently identified HiPo's or managers transitioning into more senior positions. Business school students are a frequent customer as well. That being said, we've had everyone from c-level executives at major corporations to entry-level analysts complete the course and report positive results. In our experience, most everyone benefits from dedicated coaching.

How does the 360-degree survey work?

Our enterprise 360-degree survey tool can be used (optional) to assess your performance before and after the course. Instructions on how best to administer the self-service survey are included in the introduction to the program.

Why choose WorkCoach?

Members of our founding team have coached some of the highest profile leaders in the world. Altogether our organization has over 75 years of experience delivering measurable results. As self-promoting as it might sound, if you want intensive, high-impact coaching, there's really no better place to start.

What are the main differences between the Basic and Professional programs?

Both follow the same sequence of assessments, exercises, and content. Those in the Professional program, however, are paired with a coach who works with them as they proceed through the sections. While coaches are a very valuable source of insight, self-directed people willing to challenge themselves often see comparable gains in the Basic program.

How will I communicate with my coach?

Your coach will engage you via our private coaching platform based on how you proceed through the program. Depending on your and your coach's availability, different arrangements may be possible.

Is there a fixed period of time during which you're required to complete the program?

By default, accounts are automatically locked 365 days after a user first signs in. That said, if extenuating circumstances prevent you from completing the program by that time, just email us and we'll happily unlock your account.

What kind of time commitment does the program require?

As you might expect, that depends on how much effort you choose to put into it. Most sections take anywhere from 45 minutes to 4 hours, depending on your goals. Note that as of (Q4) 2016, the program has changed to a drip format. Sections are now made available on a weekly basis because data on client performance has overwhelmingly demonstrated that to be the optimal pacing.

Is the program available in other languages besides English?

No, unfortunately at this time WorkCoach is only available in English. Because of client interest, however, we are exploring the possibility of internationalizing in the future.

How is my privacy protected?

Your privacy is one of our primary concerns here at WorkCoach. In building our platform, we've taken extensive steps to rigorously protect your information. Enterprise-grade security features are in place to guarantee your complete anonymity.

How do I learn more about the science behind WorkCoach?

When clients asked similar questions in the past, oftentimes we'd send them copies of the original reference publications. Though more recently, we've created an entire database of relevant research papers and made it available to curious customers. Some of these source documents can be publicly viewed here.

How frequently will I interact with my coach?

At a minimum, your coach will check-in with you after you complete each section to provide relevant feedback and guidance. Typically clients communicate with their coach every few days or on a weekly basis. To be clear, your coach won't be available for something like a 90-minute phone consultation every day. To a large degree, how you interact depends on what your coach and you mutually decide is best.

How are coaches matched with participants? Can I change coaches?

As of late-2016, we use a proprietary matching algorithm to ensure an optimal coach-to-client fit. And yes, requests to change coaches are very unusual but are typically able to be accommodated.
